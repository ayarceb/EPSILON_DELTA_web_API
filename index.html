Environmental Monitoring & Satellite Observations

We utilize TROPOMI satellite data to monitor atmospheric NO2 and other pollutants, supporting environmental assessments and decision-making globally.

Emission Estimation & Data Assimilation Capabilities

Our work involves emission estimation using advanced data assimilation and lightweight methods integrated into Chemical Transport Models. These tools enable precise monitoring and forecasting of air quality.

Monitoring Specific Locations Around the World

Through satellite data analysis, we offer precise environmental monitoring services for targeted locations, enhancing the ability to respond proactively to ecological challenges.
